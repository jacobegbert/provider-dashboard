Patient Portal Design Principles
🤝 Personal, not clinical
Warm ivory background, soft borders, editorial serif fonts. Feels like a private wellness journal, not a hospital portal.
✅ Task-first layout
The "Today's Focus" hero and task list are front and center — compliance starts with clarity. Patients know exactly what to do each day.
🌟 Tier recognition
Elite tier is shown in the sidebar with a gold star badge on a dark chip. Patients feel the exclusivity of their membership.
💬 Dr. connection is prominent
The message from Dr. Egbert appears directly on the dashboard with a "Reply" CTA. The relationship with the provider is central.
📊 Compliance made motivating
The sidebar shows live compliance % with a mini progress bar. It's a quiet motivator rather than a judgment. Green = momentum.
🖤 Dark hero sections
The dark today-hero and AI card create visual anchors in the warm palette. Dark + gold reads as "premium and focused" — draws the eye.
