Key Changes from Current Design
🖤 Dark sidebar
Near-black (#0A0A0A) replaces the current white sidebar. Instantly creates visual hierarchy and a "command center" feel appropriate for a provider dashboard.
🟡 Actual gold accent
Replace steel blue with warm gold (#C4993A) — the "Black Label" brand name literally calls for gold. This gives every interactive element unmistakable brand identity.
📰 Cormorant Garamond headings
Swap Outfit for Cormorant Garamond on headings. The current Outfit is modern but generic. Cormorant is editorial, authoritative, and used by luxury medical practices.
🍦 Warm cream background
Replace pure-white background with warm cream (#F7F4EE). Cards remain white, creating a subtle layer effect. Pure white backgrounds feel clinical; cream feels concierge.
🟢 Richer semantic colors
Forest green for success/active, muted amber for warnings, deep red for alerts. More sophisticated than the current flat colors — medical but not cold.
🔲 Subscription tier badges
Elite tier gets a near-black badge with gold text — premium feels premium. Standard is muted. This communicates client value at a glance in the patient list.
